About Aces:


ACES stands for the Annual Conference for Engineering Students.
ACES community started in 2004 with a main aim to decrease the gap between the academic needs and markets needs. So ACES was born to fulfill this gap.



Aces Mission:


Raising the capabilities of the engineering community
through technical, nontechnical and personal approaches
delivered through a real life exposure.


Aces Vision:


Aces has started its journey aiming to have a community of influencial leaders
who can compete in the job market and stand out of the crowd
by giving them the opportunity to increase their technical capabilities
as well as non-technical and unleash their hidden potential.
